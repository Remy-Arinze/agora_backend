# Future Plan: Global Curriculum Marketplace

## Objective
To transform Agora from a tool into an ecosystem where high-performing schools and elite teachers can monetize their curriculum expertise, and struggling schools can purchase ready-made, high-quality teaching materials.

## Context
Currently, we have a two-tier system: Agora Standard (NERDC) and School-Specific. The "Marketplace" creates a third tier: **Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Library**.

## Features

### 1. The "Creator" Dashboard
- Teachers/Admins can "Publish to Marketplace".
- Set a price in "Agora Credits".
- Upload supplementary resources (slides, worksheets, assessments) alongside the Scheme of Work.

### 2. Discovery & Rating System
- "Search by State/Region": Find curricula that are performing well in similar demographics.
- Peer Review: Verified teachers can leave reviews and success scores based on student performance metrics.
- Sample View: Allow schools to preview Week 1–2 before purchasing.

### 3. Monetization Engine
- **Agora Credits** as the primary currency.
- Revenue Split: 70% to the creator (school), 30% to the Agora platform fee.
- Instant Settlement: Credits are transferred immediately upon purchase to the school's "earnings" wallet, which can be used to pay for Agora subscriptions.

### 4. Direct Integration with Lois AI
- When a school purchases a marketplace curriculum, they can use it as a `SchemeGenerationMode` source.
- Lois AI can "merge" a marketplace curriculum with a school's existing documents to create a hybrid plan.

## Policy & Safety
- **Content Moderation**: AI-driven screening for sensitive or inappropriate content.
- **Copyright Protection**: Watermarking of PDF resources and prevention of bulk exports for non-purchased materials.
- **Quality Insurance**: A "Gold Label" awarded to curricula with a high success-to-completion ratio.
